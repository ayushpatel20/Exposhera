Corporate Overview

Exposhera Security Force

Exposhera Security Force Pvt. Ltd. is India's premier, PSARA-compliant private security agency and industrial safety equipment provider.

We are dedicated to safeguarding industrial, commercial, residential, and corporate facilities across India. Our organization blends highly trained manpower, rigorous operational standards, and modern technology to deliver custom safety covers that protect lives, assets, and reputations.

With an active deployment of over 500+ verified guards and ex-army professionals, we offer statutory-compliant protection solutions that are monitored 24/7. In addition, we supply a wide range of premium industrial safety wear and protective gear to meet comprehensive safety mandates.
