Product Description
                        












This full body harness is equipped with adjustable chest, shoulder, waist, and thigh straps to help position and restrain workers while working at height. The Class P design includes two lateral D-rings mounted at waist level for work-positioning applications, allowing workers to maintain stability while keeping both hands free.
